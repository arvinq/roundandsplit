A Tip Calculator that Makes Tipping Easy

Round & Split has three features that you'll like

Total: Whole Numbers

The app always gives you a rounded total. This makes it easy to pay (especially in cash!) and process mentally.

Split with Square速 Cash

Paying your dining partner or requesting money? The app helps you send an email to Square with the calculated amount.

Easy to Use, No Fluff

Enter the billed amount, choose from the most common tipping rates, pay the total (or copy the numbers to the receipt), and that's it!

Round & Split is available on iPhone and Android.
